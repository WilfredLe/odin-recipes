Delicious, healthy green kale smoothie (Image provided by LoveAndLemons.com)

A healthy and refreshing smooth made with: kale, dates, ginger, and orange juice!

The sweetness of the dates and gingers combine with the kale to create a refreshing and healthy drink

Ingredients

  • Kale
  • Dates
  • Ginger
  • Orange Juice
  • Sugar
  • Ice

Steps

  1. Combine kale, dates, ginger, orange juice, and sugar into blender
  2. Blend until consistency is smooth
  3. Add ice to liquid and blend
  4. Pour out and enjoy refreshing beverage!
