Korokke

Freshly cooked Korokke (Image provided by JustOneCookBook.com)

Korokke is a fried dish consisting of potatoes, onions, and ground beef!

The golden brown coating is made from the panko breadcrumbing

Ingredients

  • Russet Potatoes
  • Yellow Onions
  • Ground Beef
  • Panko Flakes

Steps

  1. Gather ingredients
  2. Peel and boil potatoes
  3. Cook beef and onions
  4. Combine potatoes, beef, and onions
  5. Cover patty in panko flakes
  6. Deep fry and serve!
