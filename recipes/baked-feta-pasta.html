Baked Feta Pasta

Freshly cooked Baked Feta Pasta (Image provided by CookingClassy.com)

Baked feta pasta is a dish only consisting of four ingredients: pasta, olive oil, feta cheese, and tomatoes!

The feta cheese and olive oil combine to make a creamy sauce to eat the pasta with!

Ingredients

  • Tomatoes
  • Pasta of choice
  • Feta cheese blocks
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. Combine tomatoes and olive oil into pan
  2. Add feta cheese blocks to pan
  3. Place pan into heated oven
  4. Wait for dish to cook
  5. Remove and serve baked pasta!
